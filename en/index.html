After a fight with his brother that ends in the loss of his kite, Exu tries to sleep, but his anger awakens a fantastic storm where reality and dream mix. To get the kite back, he must face the storm and learn to overcome his own pride.

✨ Based on the short story "Exu was afraid", from the book Tales of Exu, by Rafael Semino.
